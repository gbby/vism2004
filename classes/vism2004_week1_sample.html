Welcome to somebody's VISM2004 journal!!

Using Mozilla Thimble or an appropriate alternative (e.g. JSFiddle; raw html), students will create a "home page" for their journal, as well as a "week 1 page" on which they will share thoughts (in around 200-250 words) on:

  • where the internet came from (using the Abbate article or the CBC video for reference);
  • where the internet is going (using the Motherboard or Wired pieces for reference);
  • what "the internet" means to them.
